ABOUT
Hi there, I’m glad you found me. My name is Dacern Naidu and I like Arts.
PLURAL is a space dedicated to celebrating and sharing the richness of Malaysian cultural art. From intricate batik patterns to traditional motifs reimagined in modern forms, every piece is a reflection of our heritage and creativity. We believe art is more than just something to admire — it's a living conversation between the artist and the viewer, where inspiration flows both ways. Through PLURAL, we hope to spark curiosity, preserve tradition, and inspire new expressions rooted in culture.
Dacern Naidu.
And this is Aleeya, my creative partner on this journey.
Aleeya brings vision, detail, and a deep appreciation for Malaysian heritage to PLURAL. Her passion lies in storytelling through design — drawing from tradition while exploring modern forms of artistic expression. Together, we’ve built PLURAL not just as a showcase, but as a space for culture to breathe, connect, and evolve. Every piece we share is shaped by both of us — grounded in purpose, lifted by collaboration.
Ainul Aleeya.
