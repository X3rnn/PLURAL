Highlighting Zulfa Safeeya
My artistic journey explores a wide range of styles from expressive batik patterns and portraiture to whimsical cartoons and elegant khat calligraphy. I enjoy working with various mediums such as pen, pastel, acrylic, and watercolor, allowing each piece to take on a life of its own.
For me, art isn’t just about what’s on the surface it’s about truly understanding what lies beneath. Even the simplest artwork holds a deeper meaning, and it’s that depth that I always strive to capture in every stroke.
A whimsical gateway to a dreamy world where stars twinkle, clouds shimmer, and every meow is magic. This piece invites the viewer to escape reality, even just for a moment through the eyes of curious, mischievous cats who guard the portal to pure joy.
Zulfa Safeeya
